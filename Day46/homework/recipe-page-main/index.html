Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick omelette recipe perfect for any meal. It can be made with a variety of ingredients.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Prep: 5 minutes
  • Cook: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier omelette.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, or the oil is hot, pour in the egg mixture. Tilt the pan to ensure the egg mixture evenly coats the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set but are still slightly runny, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully fold one half over the fillings. Once the omelette is cooked through and the fillings are heated, slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy immediately: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
